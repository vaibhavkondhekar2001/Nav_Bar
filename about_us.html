Who We Are

Welcome to Amazon, where shopping is made easy! We strive to provide our customers with the widest selection of products, fast delivery through Amazon Prime, and a seamless shopping experience.

Our mission is to be Earth's most customer-centric company, where customers can find and discover anything they might want to buy online.

Our History

Amazon was founded by Jeff Bezos in 1994 as an online bookstore. Since then, it has expanded to become the largest online retailer in the world, offering a vast array of products including electronics, apparel, books, and more.

In addition to its retail business, Amazon has diversified into various other industries such as cloud computing, artificial intelligence, and entertainment streaming.
